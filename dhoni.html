About MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, fondly known as Mahi, is one of the greatest captains in cricket history. He led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ODI World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy.

A calm finisher, brilliant wicketkeeper, and tactical genius, Dhoni also brought massive success to Chennai Super Kings in the IPL.
